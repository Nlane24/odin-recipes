Superfood Berry-Green Smoothy

Recipe Description

This smoothy is packed with superfoods to provide you plenty or protein, fiber, antioxidants, and vitamin C to keep you going! You can easily customize it to add your favorite fruits, non-dairy milks, and everything else you love. It's quick to make in only 10 minutes and is a great way for you and your family to start your day!

Ingredients

  • Almond milk
  • Clementines
  • Raspberries
  • Spinach
  • Chia seeds
  • Flax seeds
  • Blueberries
  • Your preferred protein powder

Steps

  1. Add the fruits, seeds, and spinach to a blender.
  2. Pour over the almond milk or whichever milk you are using.
  3. Blend on high until smooth consistency.
  4. Pour into a glass and optionally add a garnish for presentation.
  5. Enjoy!
