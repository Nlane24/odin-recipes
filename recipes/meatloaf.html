The Best Meatloaf Recipe

Recipe Description

Meatloaf is America. This traditional meal is as embedded in American culture along with apple pie and burgers. The recipe is fairly simple and customizable to your family's liking. What really makes this recipe stand-out and makes it so American is ketchup glaze. Heinz 57 is is the original, but use any ketchup you have!

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ground Beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Buttery Crackers
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Sautee the onions and garlic in a pan.
  3. Combine the meat, onions and garlic, everything in a large bowl.
  4. Transfer this mixutre to a loaf pan and bake for 40 mins.
  5. Remove from over and glaze the top with the ketchup and cook for another 10 mins.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
